Core Repair & Maintenance

Emergency AC Repair

Compressor failures, tripped breakers, weak airflow, and systems that freeze overnight in humid conditions. We diagnose first, then repair fast so you’re not stuck without cooling.

Furnace & Heat Repair

Ignition problems, blower issues, and safety checks for gas and electric heating. Houston winters are mild until a front drops overnight—we keep heat ready.

Preventive Maintenance

Coil cleaning, condensate drain clearing, filter schedules, and electrical checks that prevent midsummer breakdowns when every crew in Harris County is booked.

Indoor Air Quality

Filtration upgrades, humidity control options, and duct hygiene for homes that stay closed up with the AC running for months.

Smart Thermostats

Install and configure thermostats that manage long cooling seasons without short-cycling or overcooling empty rooms.

Ductwork & Airflow

Seal and rebalance duct systems that waste cool air in attics—especially common in ranch plans along the FM 1960 and SH 249 corridors.

New System Sales & Installation

When repair costs stack up against a newer, properly sized system, we don’t stop at “try another part.” We sell and install complete AC and heating systems, including heat pumps and high-efficiency options suited to ASHRAE Climate Zone 2A.
